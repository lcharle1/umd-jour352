Bio

Bonne Café is coffee shop offering great wifi, a warm environment, and amazing coffee. The business has been passed down six generations of Loufierre's. Starting in the countryside of France. Gerald Loufierre migrated to America to provide for his family and brought with him what he knows best, coffee. A century later the business is proudly running.
